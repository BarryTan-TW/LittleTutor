# LittleTutor
LittleTutor is an Android app designed as a learning companion for primary school students, focusing on Chinese and English language study. It helps children study with ease while giving parents clear insight into learning progress.
The app combines OCR, reading assistance, handwriting practice, quizzes, and progress tracking into one integrated learning system — just like having a personal tutor at home.

✨ Key Features
📸 Textbook Digitization
Scan textbook pages using the camera
Convert images into editable text via OCR
Save and organize content as digital textbooks

✍️ Vocabulary & Phrase Extraction
Select words or phrases directly from textbook content
Build personalized vocabulary and phrase lists for each lesson
Automatically generate quiz content from selected items

🔊 Reading & Writing Support
Read aloud textbook content with text‑to‑speech
Learn correct stroke order for Chinese characters
Generate related words and phrases for vocabulary learning

📝 Quiz & Assessment
Conduct quizzes on single or multiple lessons
Test writing and recognition of words and phrases
Flexible quiz scope configuration

📊 Learning Analysis & History
Save scores for each quiz by lesson
Analyze mistakes and highlight areas that need improvement
Maintain a complete learning history for long‑term tracking

👨‍👩‍👧 Multi‑Student Support
Support multiple students within one family
Organize records by student name, subject, grade, and lesson
Suitable for different ages and learning levels

🎯 Target Users
Primary school students (elementary level)
Parents looking for structured at‑home study support
Educators interested in digital learning tools

🌱 Vision
LittleTutor aims to make learning structured, interactive, and stress‑free, turning textbooks into smart learning materials and helping children grow with confidence.

# The original ideal
我想要一個 android app 類似國小生的國語與英文的陪讀老師
這個 app 可以
1. 透過照片掃描,把課文轉變成文字,然後存檔起來變成課本
2. 瀏覽課文的時候,可以透過畫面圈選的方式,把每課需要考的單字或詞選出來,變成單字和詞的考試本
3. 可以朗讀課文,並教導單字筆順,也可以透過單字列出造詞
4. 提供小考功能,考小朋友會不會寫單字,小考範圍可以指定單課或是多課內容
5. 每課的小考評分保存下來,統計並列出需要補強的地方
6. 所有的成績紀錄起來變成history
7. 透過學生名字來支援一個家庭多個小孩或是一個學生多個不同課目或是不同年級
